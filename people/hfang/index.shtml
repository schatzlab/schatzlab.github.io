Han Fang
Han Fang received his M.S. in Applied Math & Statistics at Stony Brook University (SBU) and received his B.S. in Optical Informatics at Sun Yat-sen University, China. He is currently a PhD student in Applied Math & Statistics at SBU, supervised by Dr. Michael Schatz and Dr. Gholson Lyon at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory (CSHL). His research interests intersect at machine learning, statistics, and genomics, with the goal of understanding underlying patterns of complex data.
